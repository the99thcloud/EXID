市場洞察 01

Market Insight 01

壓倒性的參與意願

Overwhelming Willingness to Attend

這份期待，無需等待

This Anticipation Can't Wait

數據揭示了一個驚人的事實：高達 93% 的粉絲表達了「高度參與意願」。這不是一個需要從零培養、等待發酵的市場，而是一個能量積蓄已久、只需一個火花就能引爆的「即時變現」市場。這意味著票房基本盤極其穩固，宣傳成本效益極高，任何額外的路人粉絲都將是錦上添花。

The data reveals a striking fact: a staggering 93% of fans have expressed a "high willingness to attend." This is not a market that needs to be cultivated from scratch; it's a "ready-to-monetize" market with long-accumulated energy, needing only a spark to ignite. This indicates an extremely solid box office foundation and high cost-effectiveness for promotion.

市場洞察 02

Market Insight 02

強勁的消費能力

Strong Spending Power

價值，是對等待的最好回答

Value is the Best Answer to Waiting

超過 66% 的粉絲願意接受 NT$4,200 以上的票價，這不僅反映了粉絲群的經濟實力，更是對 EXID 七年等待的高度價值認可。這組數據為營收策略提供了明確方向：設立 NT$5,800 以上的 VIP 票區不僅可行，更是粉絲所期待的。將高吸引力的福利與之綑綁，將能有效驅動消費升級，創造遠超預期的營收潛力。

Over 66% of fans are willing to accept ticket prices above NT$4,200, reflecting not only their financial strength but also a high valuation for the seven-year wait for EXID. This data provides a clear direction for revenue strategy: creating a VIP section priced above NT$5,800 is not only feasible but expected by fans, promising significant revenue potential.

Market Insight 03

極高的粉絲忠誠度

Extremely High Fan Loyalty

時間淬鍊的鑽石級鐵粉

Diamond-Tier Fans Forged Over Time

高達 82% 為追隨超過四年的資深粉絲，其中更有 60% 是超過七年的「鑽石級鐵粉」。這不是短暫的潮流，而是一個經過逆行奇蹟與漫長等待所淬鍊、情感連結極深的成熟社群。他們不僅是票房的基石，更是最佳的「品牌大使」，會自發地維護藝人聲譽並推廣活動，形成正向的口碑循環，同時也是周邊商品最穩定的消費主力。

A remarkable 82% of fans have been supporters for over four years, with 60% being "diamond-tier" fans for over seven years. This is not a fleeting trend but a mature community with deep emotional ties, forged through chart comebacks and long waits. They are the cornerstone of ticket sales and the most stable consumers of merchandise.

市場洞察 04

Market Insight 04

互動體驗價值最高

High Value on Interactive Experiences

渴望的，是真實的連結

A Craving for Real Connection

粉絲對「擊掌(86%)」和「團體合照(79%)」的渴望，明確揭示了「稀缺的互動體驗」是粉絲最願意付費的項目。這為票價策略提供了清晰指引：將最高價票區的核心價值，直接錨定在「與偶像的近距離互動」上。相較於製作實體周邊，高品質的互動福利執行成本相對更易控制，卻能創造最高的粉絲滿意度與利潤空間。

The strong desire for "Hi-Touch" (86%) and "Group Photo" (79%) reveals that "scarce interactive experiences" are what fans are most willing to pay for. This provides a clear guide for pricing strategy: anchor the core value of the highest-priced tickets to "close-up interaction with idols" to maximize fan satisfaction and profit margins.

Market Insight 05

黃金消費客群輪廓

Profile of Prime Consumers

精準的市場目標

A Precise Target Market

粉絲主力為 25-34 歲 (佔 70%)、且集中在北部地區 (佔 68%)。這堪稱是「行銷人的夢想藍圖」。這群有穩定經濟基礎的上班族，集中在交通最便利的地區，代表著行銷資源可以高度集中，用最有效率的方式觸及核心客群。這也為場地選擇與活動時間安排（週末為佳）提供了明確、無懸念的決策依據。

The fan base is primarily composed of individuals aged 25-34 (70%) and concentrated in the Northern region (68%). This is a "marketer's dream scenario": working professionals with stable incomes in an easily accessible area. This allows for highly focused marketing efforts and provides a clear basis for venue selection and scheduling (weekends preferred).

結論與最終建議

Conclusion & Final Recommendations

綜合歷史成功經驗與最新市場數據，所有指標均明確顯示：此刻正是 EXID 重返台灣市場的黃金時機。 這是一個無需教育、無需等待、無需承擔巨大風險的市場。粉絲的熱情與消費力已準備就緒，等待的只是一個值得信賴的主辦方將其點燃。

最終建議： 立即啟動專案，鎖定台北國際會議中心 (TICC)。這不僅是交通便利、音場與視野俱佳的專業場地，更是 EXID 已有成功經驗的福地。回顧 2016 年的粉絲見面會，當時不僅票房開出紅盤，高價位的 VIP 票券更是瞬間秒殺，證明了粉絲對此場地的高度認可。選擇 TICC 不僅是最佳選擇，更是複製成功經驗的保證。售票策略建議採「階段性開賣」模式：優先開啟視野絕佳、粉絲黏著度最高的二樓約 1,500 席座位。若能迅速完售，不僅能提前確保損益平衡、將風險降至最低，更能創造「秒殺」的媒體話題，為後續三、四樓座位的銷售奠定強心針。同時，建議對最高票價區域採「實名制」，有效抵制黃牛，保障核心粉絲權益。我們預期這不僅會是一場快速完售的演出，更將成為 2025 年 K-POP 市場最具話題性的成功案例之一。

Combining historical success with current market data, all indicators clearly show: now is the golden opportunity for EXID to return to the Taiwanese market. This is a market that requires no education, no waiting, and no significant risk. The fans' passion and spending power are ready, waiting only for a trustworthy organizer to ignite them.

Final Recommendation: Immediately initiate the project and secure the Taipei International Convention Center (TICC). It is not only a professional venue with convenient transportation and excellent acoustics but also a proven ground of success for EXID. Recalling the 2016 fan meeting, not only did ticket sales flourish, but high-priced VIP tickets sold out instantly, proving fans' high approval of this venue. Choosing TICC is the best choice and a guarantee of replicating success. A "phased ticket release" strategy is recommended: prioritize opening the second-floor seats (~1,500), which offer the best views and cater to the most loyal fans. A quick sell-out would ensure profitability, minimize risk, and create media buzz, boosting sales for the remaining sections. Additionally, implementing a "real-name system" for the highest-priced tickets is advised to combat scalping and protect core fans. We expect this to be not just a sold-out show, but one of the most talked-about success stories in the 2025 K-POP market.
